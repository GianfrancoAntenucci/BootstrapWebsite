Our Services

Lasik

We have extensive experience performing customized, all-laser LASIK surgery, helping patients restore their vision and eliminating the need for most eyeglass prescriptions. The procedure is safe, fast, and extraordinarily effective.

Refractive

We recommend refractive lens exchange (RLE) as an alternative to LASIK for patients who want freedom from glasses. RLE's advanced lens implant options can provide clear, crisp vision at all distances, eliminating the vision changes that usually occur as we age.

Cataracts

Restore cataract-related vision loss at our practice. We are leading ophthalmic surgeons and have performed thousands of successful cataract surgeries.
