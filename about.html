About Us

We are Medical and Surgical Ophthalmologists with specializations in refractive cataract surgery and laser vision correction. Surgery is performed at our clinic, a leading-edge surgery center dedicated exclusively to eye procedures.

This modern facility has all of the latest technology available to assist us and provide you with a successful outcome. Every effort will be made to make sure you are comfortable and informed throughout your stay.

We believe care doesn't just look like surgical skill and a successful procedure; it also looks like helping a patient choose the right option with a balance of education, transparency, and genuine interest in their wellbeing.
